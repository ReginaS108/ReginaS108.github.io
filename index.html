Who am I

Junrong Sheng is a PhD candidate in the Department of Sociology at the University of Notre Dame. She studies gender inequality, family relationships, and intra-household dynamics in East Asian societies, particularly in China. She is adept at both quantitative and qualitative research methods, and dedictated to obtaining valid answers to her research questions through mixed-method approaches. She teaches quantitative classes in her current position.
